Our Story

Preserving tradition one pickle at a time

Hello Pickle Lovers!

We’re just two corporate creatures crunching numbers by day 🧑‍💻 and crushing lal mirch by night 🌶 — all in the name of achaar!

What started as midnight cravings 🌙 turned into a full-blown pickle passion project. But let’s be real — the real boss here is our mom (aka the Achaar OG) 👵, whose magic hands and secret recipes are the heart and soul of every jar ❤‍🔥.

Straight from our kitchen to your plate 🍽, these pickles are desi, dramatic, and dripping with nostalgia. No preservatives 🚫, no shortcuts ⏩ — just maa-approved, mood-uplifting, lip-smacking goodness 😋.

So grab a spoon 🥄 (or your fingers — we don’t judge) and let’s get pickled! 🥭✨
